" collection: portfolio --- The goal of this project is to classify political blogs as either liberal or conservative using the Polblogs dataset. The dataset used is the one recorded and analysed in this paper proposed in 2005, which contains a collection of the political blogs that were most active in 2004 in the run-up to the US presidential election. This dataset not only includes metadata about individual blogs but also captures the hyperlinks between them, forming a graph structure that reflects the networked nature of political discourse.
